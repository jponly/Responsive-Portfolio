About Me

I grew up in the heart of Sillicon Valley during the very beginning. My father was a part of bringing it to what it is today, a global existential being embedded in our psyche. I always loved fantasy, so I enrolled in film school and shipped to southern California to pursue film. The filmmaking process is intense and fascinating. The amount of people, hard work and funding it takes to produce a film was eye-opening. Though it was great to be a part of the process, I wanted to immerse myself into something creative that reached out and touched our everyday lives. The desire to utilize a skillset everyday and evolve that

skillset, adding new additional methods to a markup, styling or programming code within every single present moment to take a website to new heights is why I want to be a Web Developer. I truly believe Web Development can ascend my imagination to a vast and broader Universe.
